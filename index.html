About US

JS Cleaning Service was founded through the hard work and dedication of Josiel and Sueli, a Brazilian couple with a deep commitment to family and community. As parents of three, they understand how difficult it can be to keep a home clean and comfortable while managing daily responsibilities. Wanting to help other families enjoy a fresh, stress-free living space, they turned their passion for cleaning into a business. In 2022, they launched JS Cleaning Service with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, reliable cleaning services with a personal touch.
For Josiel and Sueli, cleaning is more than just tidying up—it’s about creating a healthier, happier home for their clients. They take pride in their attention to detail, treating every home as if it were their own. Whether it’s routine cleaning, a deep refresh, or a post-construction cleanup, they bring the same level of care and dedication to every job. Their growing business is built on trust, reliability, and a genuine passion for helping people enjoy a cleaner, more comfortable space.

Our Services

JS Cleaning Service is dedicated to keeping your home spaces spotless and well-maintained. Whether you need a home standard/deep or post-construction cleaning, we’re here to help. Check out our wide range of services:

Standard Cleaning

Our Standard Cleaning service provides routine maintenance to keep your home fresh and tidy. It includes dusting, vacuuming, mopping, and general surface cleaning to ensure a consistently clean space.

Deep Cleaning

Our Deep Cleaning service goes beyond regular cleaning, tackling built-up dirt, dust, and grime in every corner of your home. Perfect for seasonal refreshes or when your space needs extra care.

Move-In/Move-Out Cleaning

Our Move-In/Move-Out Cleaning service provides a thorough deep clean to prepare your home for new occupants or ensure you leave your previous space spotless. We focus on every detail, making the transition smooth and stress-free.

Post-Construction Cleaning

Our Post-Construction Cleaning service ensures your newly built or renovated space is spotless and ready to use. We remove dust, debris, and construction residue, leaving your property clean, safe, and move-in ready.
